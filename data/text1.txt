Holi arrives like a burst of color after a long, quiet winter, filling the air with laughter, music, and the sweet scent of gujiyas frying in kitchens. Streets turn into vibrant canvases where friends chase each other with handfuls of गुलाल, smearing pinks, blues, and yellows across smiling faces. There’s a carefree joy in the chaos—water balloons flying, drums beating in the distance, and strangers becoming friends for a day. As the sun begins to set, the colors fade but the warmth lingers, leaving behind memories of togetherness, forgiveness, and the simple happiness of celebrating life in its brightest shades.
